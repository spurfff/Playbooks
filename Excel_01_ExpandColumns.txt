#### AutoFit Excel Column Width ####

1.) Select the columns you'd like to expand (i.e. shift-click, ctrl-click)

2.) Go to the Home tab in the excel ribbon

3.) In the "Cells Group" you'll find the "Format dropdown" menu

4.) From the dropdown menu select "Autofit Column Width"

5.) The cells should now be adusted to reveal the entire text of each cell
